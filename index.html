    Our Mission

    Our vision for Cyber Carnival is to create a platform that brings together cybersecurity enthusiasts from all over the world to share knowledge, expertise, and ideas. We strive to be a global leader in promoting cybersecurity awareness and education, and to provide opportunities for professionals and students to network, learn, and collaborate.

    Our Plan

    The carnival featured home-grown cybersecurity games, activities, prizes, and other treats. More than 1,000 students, staff, and faculty attend the event.

    Our Vision

    Our mission for Cyber Carnival is to organize an annual event that showcases the latest trends and innovations in cybersecurity. We aim to provide a diverse range of sessions and workshops that cater to all levels of expertise, from beginners to seasoned professionals. Our goal is to facilitate learning and growth in the cybersecurity community, while promoting a culture of ethical and responsible hacking practices.
